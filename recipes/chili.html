5 Ingredient Chili

Bowl of Chili

This is a very easy recipe that only requires 5 ingredients. It also takes very little time to make. Perfect for a busy family!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 15 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (15 oz) can chili beans, not drained
  • 2 Tbsps chili powder
